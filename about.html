About Us

Systems thinking for aerial data.

GroundTruth Systems exists to give project teams decision-grade information they can trust. We apply a systems-engineering mindset to every mission so data moves from the field to your plan without friction.

Mission

Deliver high-trust aerial mapping, monitoring, and change analysis so construction, development, and property teams keep work on schedule.

Principles

  • Plan the work with documented SOPs.
  • Fly disciplined routes with redundancy.
  • Process data with calibrated workflows.
  • Communicate clearly and stay accountable.

Safety & Compliance

Part 107 certified. Insured. Repeatable.

Licensed Pilots

Every operator is Part 107 certified and trained on construction site safety etiquette.

Airspace & Risk

We handle authorizations, NOTAM checks, and insurance certificates before stepping on site.

Data Custody

Flights, settings, and processing parameters are logged so results are defensible and repeatable.

SOP Discipline

Checklists govern pre-flight, capture, and delivery so nothing relies on memory.

Leadership

Operational pedigree meets field pragmatism.

GroundTruth Systems is led by systems engineers with backgrounds in command-and-control software, enterprise IT, and commercial UAV operations. Field teams blend aviation discipline with construction awareness, giving clients a partner who speaks both technology and jobsite reality.

Coverage

Florida headquarters. Southeast deployments.

We stage missions across Florida, Georgia, Alabama, and the Carolinas with vetted partners for surge projects nationwide. Need support elsewhere? We coordinate with our trusted network and keep GroundTruth QA on every deliverable.
